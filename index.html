Big Bash Cricket in Australia

The Big Bash is a Twenty-Twenty cricket tournament in Australia. The tournament started in 2011 and has seen the best of the best Australian and International cricket players. Many Australian players have made a name for themselves playing in the tournament. Here is an overview of the tournament and legendary players that are the best in the league for their given skill.


BBL: An Overview

In the Big Bash there are 8 teams:
Melbourne Stars
Melbourne Renegades
Sydney Thunder
Sydney Sixers
Brisbane Heat
Perth Scorchers
Hobart Hurricanes
Adelaide Strikers
