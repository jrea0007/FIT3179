Big Bash Cricket in Australia


The Big Bash is a Twenty-Twenty cricket tournament in Australia. The tournament started in 2011 and has had the best of the best Australian and International cricket players play in the tournament. Many Australian players have made a name for themselves playing resulting in callups to the Australian Cricket Team. Here is an overview of the tournament and legendary players that are the best in the league for their given skill.







Best Players in the BBL

BBL: An Overview

The Big Bash League (BBL) was started in 2011 and has ran annually in Australia from November to January.

The BBL contains 8 teams:

Melbourne Stars
Melbourne Renegades
Sydney Thunder
Sydney Sixers
Brisbane Heat
Perth Scorchers
Hobart Hurricanes
Adelaide Strikers

The Perth Scorchers are the most successful team in the competition, with titles in BBL03 (2013-2014), BBL04 (2014-2015), BBL06 (2016-2017) and BBL11 (2021-2022).
